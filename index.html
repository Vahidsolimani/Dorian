Ideal
Growing Conditions

The Eastern Monthong grows in the dry climate and mineral-rich soil of Rayong, Chanthaburi, and Trat provinces, giving the variety its distinct flavor profile.

unique
Taste and Texture

The Eastern Monthong durian is renowned for its rich, creamy texture and sweet, aromatic flavor, making it highly sought after among durian enthusiasts.

limited
Harvest Window

Harvested only from April to June, the Eastern Monthong is highly prized. We make the distinctive flavor of this seasonal indulgence available to you year-round.

Frequently
Asked Questions

What are the Bing Qi Guo and Xue Bao made of, and do they contain any dairy, sugar, or food additives?

+

The Bing Qi Guo and Xue Bao contain no dairy, sugar, or food additives. They are made from 100% Eastern Monthong durian.

What is the difference between the Xue Bao and Bing Qi Guo?

+

The Xue Bao is made from 100% Eastern Monthong puree, giving it a soft and smooth texture. The Bing Qi Guo is made from 100% Eastern Monthong pulp, offering a more authentic, textured experience.

Are the Bing Qi Guo and Xue Bao vegan?

+

Absolutely, the Bing Qi Guo and Xue Bao are both vegan! They are made from 100% Eastern Monthong durian with no animal-derived ingredients.

How should I store the Bing Qi Guo/Xue Bao?

+

Both the Bing Qi Guo and Xue Bao should be stored at -18°C or lower. We recommend finishing them after removing from the freezer. Frequent thawing and refreezing cause ice particles to form, which will affect their taste and texture.

What are the health benefits to eating durian?

+

Durian is highly nutritious, packed with B vitamins, vitamin C, and dietary fiber. Its high carbohydrate content also provides an excellent energy boost.
